Bio

I'm a Ph.D. student of Department of Computer Science and Engineering, HKUST, supervised by Prof. Wei Wang and Prof. Xiaowen Chu. I am also fortunate to be advised by Prof. Huaicheng Li (Virginia Tech), Dr. Cheng Wang (Alibaba Group), and Dr. Baotong Lu (Microsoft Research).

My research focuses on high-performance, resource-efficient GPU cloud platforms. Specifically, I'm interested in GPU virtualization, GPU memory system, and next-generation GPU architecture.
